About Me

My journey has been anything but conventional, but that’s exactly what gives me an edge. I started with an **Associate’s Degree** in Culinary Arts, which, surprisingly, taught me far more than just cooking—it ingrained in me business fundamentals, **logistics, process optimization, and attention to detail**.

From there, I enlisted in the **U.S. Navy**, where I worked as a **Signal Analyst**. My job? **Deciphering radar signals from raw numerical data** to detect potential threats in real-time. I mastered pattern recognition, high-pressure decision-making, and the ability to **manage complex technical systems under extreme conditions**. Later, I became an **Electronic Warfare Supervisor**, responsible for tactical defense strategies and overseeing advanced military tech. Following my service, I pivoted into the world of **data analytics, programming, and operations management**. At the **Greenville Police Department**, I handle **records management, database corrections, and video evidence processing**—essentially acting as the department’s unofficial IT expert. I also manage complex software systems and assist officers with tech-related challenges. Meanwhile, I built and ran a **successful digital business**, generating over **$4,500 in sales** through **SEO-driven content, market analysis, and copywriting**. I analyze trends, craft high-performing content, and optimize engagement strategies to maximize conversions. I’ve also self-taught **Python, JavaScript, SQL, and Tableau**—leveraging my technical knowledge to **develop dashboards, automate processes, and extract insights from data**. Additionally, I’ve built **three gaming PCs from scratch**, troubleshooting both software and hardware issues, further strengthening my problem-solving skills in IT and networking. My skill set is a unique blend of **data-driven decision-making, operational efficiency, and digital strategy**. I thrive on solving problems, improving systems, and using technology to drive meaningful impact.
